Time & Premium Perception in Eyewear

Luxottica Group

Luxottica Group commissioned a study to inform strategic insight to rebrand and reposition the discount aggregator Sunglass Hut as more premium across Europe, UK and US. As the largest eyewear conglomerate in the world with a large portfolio of brands, we needed to evolve premium perception without cannibalizing sales from their other brands, Persol and Ray-Ban.

UX Redesign & Content Strategy

The brand could credibly shift premium perception by embracing storytelling cues of leading Aggregator Mavericks in e-commerce and Eyewear Conglomerates. Overall the redesign and editorial strategy needed to reflect trends in the new fashion landscape including a shift toward Gender Neutrality in styling, content and UX design.

1.
EMERGENT FASHION

To credibly leverage the brand's Low Fidelity DNA and bring the image up to date with innovations in fashion, we implimented the following design and editorial changes:


  • - Gender Neutrality in Styling & Content
  • - Less Rounded or Serif Typefaces
  • - Minimal use of Pastel Colorways
  • - Bolder Copywriting + Creative Risk

2.

TIME SENSITIVE CONTENT

Celebrating "Agreggator-ness" means to celebrate Quantity, Speed and Seriality. To create a virtual sense of scarcity to raise premium perception, we recommended the following editorial changes:


  • - Time Sensitive + Livestream Content
  • - Constantly Refreshed Product
  • - “Just Dropped”, “Runway” “Sold Out” labeling
  • - Quantification + Numbers as a design element

3.

BRAND CURATION & EXPERTISE

As Luxottica Group leads Eyewear trends from behind the scenes due to it's outsized impact on the industry, we recommended they:


  • - Repackage industry intelligence as trend content
  • - Leverage their sponsorship of London Fashion Week
  • - Develop collections X Palace, Planet I, Palm Angels
  • - Revamp the homepage with Influencer IG feeds
