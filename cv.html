MARIAN ST. LAURENT

Digital Strategy & Content Innovation

Since 2004 I have consulted internationally as a communications specialist providing cultural insight and design and marketing semiotics to inform future-facing communications strategies and innovation routes for clients in tech, entertainment, luxury, fashion, transport and consumer goods. My approach to innovation is informed by a deep understanding of the impact of technological change on storytelling trends and media aesthetics impacting brand perception and audience expectations.

Old Stories, New Technologies

A lifelong passion for understanding the impact of technology, visual culture and mass communications on societal change has led to a career in high level strategic development. Working directly with clients as an expert partner on global identity projects within a variety of creative environments has made me a valuable contributor to new product development and methodology innovation for digital media agencies, brand consultances and market research firms who have had to evolve their approach to insight and creative services to stay ahead of changing business models.
