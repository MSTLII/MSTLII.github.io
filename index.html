OLD STORIES/ NEW TECHNOLOGIES

I have worked internationally as a specialist in cultural insight and semiotics, developing immersive brand experiences for clients in media, luxury, consumer goods and transport sectors since 2004.

I develop creative strategies for innovation that leverage the possibilities of new technologies. My approach to creative strategy & innovation is informed by an intuitive and historically grounded understanding of the impact of technological change on evolving storytelling trends and consumer perception.

CREATIVE STRATEGY & INNOVATION

POWERED BY CULTURE

I have been commissioned to conduct global studies to inform guidelines and innovation routes for transport interiors, new media product development, flavour and fragrance innovation to packaging design.

Genre-specific studies I have conducted to track trends in Television and film have been syndicated for major American media, entertainment and news clients.
